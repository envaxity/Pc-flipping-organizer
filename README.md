# FlipForge

A lightweight website for organizing a PC building and flipping business.

## What it does

- Tracks builds in progress and sold systems
- Tracks individual parts inventory and expected resale value
- Tracks buyer leads and current offers
- Calculates simple profit and pipeline totals automatically
- Saves your data in the browser with `localStorage`

## How to use it

1. Open [index.html](C:\Users\hunte\Documents\New project\index.html) in your browser.
2. Use `Add Build`, `Add Part`, and `Add Lead` to enter your business data.
3. Refresh safely because the page stores entries in the browser on that device.
